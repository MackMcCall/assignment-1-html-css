Travelogue: Bora Bora

Overview:

Bora Bora is a small island in French Polynesia known for its crystal-clear turquoise lagoon, dramatic volcanic peaks, and luxurious overwater bungalows. Often called the “Pearl of the Pacific,” it is a popular destination for honeymoons and travelers seeking relaxation and natural beauty. The island blends stunning scenery with a laid-back Polynesian culture.



Must See Spots:

  1. Mount Otemanu
  2. Bora Bora Lagoon
  3. Matira Beach
  4. Coral Gardens
  5. Vaitape Village

Visitor Tips:

  • Book activities and accommodations well in advance, especially during peak travel seasons.
  • Pack reef-safe sunscreen to help protect the coral ecosystem.
  • Plan for transportation by boat, as many resorts are located on small surrounding islets.
  • Bring light, breathable clothing suitable for warm tropical weather year-round.
